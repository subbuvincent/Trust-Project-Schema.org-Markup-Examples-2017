There are moments every day when we say Thank You to someone.

While some thank yous are small and sincere, others are more poignant.

Weve partnered with Cadbury Roses to find out when people last said a truly meaningful Thank You.

Today's round-up of reader messages kicks off with a football 'Thank You' from Thomas in Bromley, London...

I have to say Thank You to Jose Mourinho for making me laugh so hard. His shaking hands with everyone from the Stoke staff apart from their manager Mark Hughes might quite possibly be the comedy moment of the entire football season, and were only in September. Its going to be hard to beat.

Thomas, Bromley

A few minutes ago I was having a meltdown as I managed to accidentally delete a whole bunch of files at work. Thankfully a lovely lady came to the rescue and managed to retrieve them all. I was so grateful!

Jenny, Scotland

I thanked a friend and neighbour by buying them lunch, and then giving them a lift to the airport after they had helped me choose a new second-hand car. I really wanted to show my appreciation.

Ron, Manchester

For me, its a friend who I thanked for looking after my dog one evening when I was stuck and had to attend a last-minute get-together with old friends visiting London. It was a great evening and it would have been such a shame if I had missed the reunion.

Paola Thatcher, Streatham

When my neighbour took me to a show at the Edinburgh Book Festival. It was so kind of them to think of me and I had a fantastic time it was the highlight of my week.

Lewis, Edinburgh

I went to Colombia during the summer holidays and my mums friend gave me a dress she had made especially for me. I was very grateful because I knew how much work was involved. So when I said Thank You to her I really, really meant it!

Raphaela, London

The last time I really thanked someone was my girlfriend Michelle after she helped me out when I was in dire straits! She kept my fridge stocked with all my favourite food when I was short of money. Ill never forget her generosity.

Pete, Birmingham

Mos our golden boy

A big Thank You to Mo Farah for some incredible moments. His career has been nothing short of inspirational.

Ill never forget watching his glorious double triumph at the London 2012 Olympics. Thanks for the memories!

Phillip Norman, Hampshire

  • Cadbury Roses has been helping people say Thank You for almost 80 years! Whether its a small token of appreciation or a gift to mark a more poignant occasion, it is the perfect way to show gratitude.
